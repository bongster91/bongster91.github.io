Recipe of the Day: Grace Bong

  • 1 slab of Korean American
  • 1 jug of LGBTQIA+
  • 1 julienned female
  • 1 sprinkling of bartender/barista
  • 1 dash of wittiness

Take your slab of Korean American, slather on that LGBTQIA and bake for 40min at 400F. Combine your julienned female with some bartener/barista and a dash of wittiness into a large bowl and toss. Serve your slab hot and sizzling with a side of cool seasoned julienne.
